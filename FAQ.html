FAQs

How do I place an order?

FACEBOOK ORDER

  • Go to Dacillo Carbon Parts FB Page @DerDacillo06.
  • Message us on what you are ordering.
  • Finalize your transaction.
  • We offer same day delivery with LALAMOVE if your order is available on our stock and if not wait atleast 7 to 14 days to create your order and it will arrive at your doorstep.

SHOPPEE ORDER

  • Go to Dacillo Carbon Parts shoppee https://shopee.ph/dacillocarbon or open the app and search dacillocarbon
  • On the product page, select Buy Now.
  • Select preferred variation option (if applicable).
  • select Buy Now.
  • Select preferred Shipping Option (if necessary).
  • Use Shopee Voucher and Shopee Coins (optional).
  • Select preferred Payment Option.
  • Place Order.

WEBSITE ORDER

  • Go to the SHOP section & browse through selection of available products
  • Add & manage desired items into your cart
  • Please double check the items in your cart as we would not be accepting cancellation of orders once your order is submitted/confirmed.
  • Proceed to checkout and fill out the form before your screen
  • Modes of payment: Debit Card / Credit Card or Gcash / Cash on Delivery
  • Wait for an email confirmation from our online sales rep and your orders will be prepared and will be shipped soon


What courier do you use when shipping items within the country?

We use LBC and JNT express for transactions outside or within Metro Manila, and LALAMOVE for same day orders.



How much is the shipping fee?

The shipping fee will depend on your quantity of your order(s) and its weight. The starting price of the shipping fee will be 50PHP and will increase depending on the weight of your item(s) and location.



How many days will it take to receive my order(s)?

You can expect to have your orders after 7-14 working days from the date of receiving your email confirmation.

You may track your orders through the email confirmation that will be sent to you.



I still haven’t received my shipment. What do I do with this?

What we can do is help you guys track your parcels and monitor its current location once the orders are shipped out. - but with regards to the date on when you’ll receive the items ordered is already out of our control. For this matter, you can verify and ask for a follow-up of the parcel with the chosen courier.



What is your Return & Exchange Policy?

Generally, only defective items or wrong order/s sent by our staff may be exchanged.

Once orders are confirmed, we cannot cancel it anymore. We do not accept refunds.



Do you guys accept international orders?

Yes, with LBC courier we can ship out our product internationally

Does the product similar to each other?

At Dacillo Carbon Parts, we take pride in crafting carbon fiber products that are a testament to the skilled hands of our artisans. We understand that, like any handmade creation, slight variations may occur from one piece to another. What makes each product truly unique is that, despite similarities in design, the process of creation is distinct for each item, ensuring that no two are exactly alike. Occasionally, you may find that stock parts fit differently, but please rest assured that this is a characteristic of the original stock, not our company's craftsmanship. Our commitment to excellence remains unwavering as we continue to deliver exceptional, handcrafted carbon fiber products for our valued customers.
